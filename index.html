Jernej Grlj🔊

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Mathematics at the University of Southern California. I am advised by Aaron Lauda and partly supported by the Simons Collaboration on New Structures in Low-Dimensional Topology.

I work at the interface of higher representation theory, low-dimensional topology, and quantum information with interests in new structures in higher representation theory and the interplay amongst these fields.
Currently I am mostly thinking about foams, structures on the categorified quantum groups, (spectral) Khovanov homology, and its application to quantum computing.
